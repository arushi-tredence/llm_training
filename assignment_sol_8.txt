Overview: 

Step 1: Go to https://github.com/oobabooga/text-generation-webui and download the repo.
Step 2: Extract the .ZIP File.
Step 3: Run the start_windows batch file.
Step 4: Go to ai.meta.com/llama/ and download the model.
Step 5: Fill the details and submit. Models will be sent over email.
Step 6: Go to HuggingFace and open meta-llama/Llama-2-7b
Step 7: Fill details and submit request. Grant will be sent over email.
Step 8: Download the required model.
Step 9: Copy the model name and paste it on the oobabooga webpage.
Step 10: Run the model.
